My story

I've always had an interest in programming. From a very young age, I've had the ability to learn many different things by myself, including a bit of HTML and JavaScript.

Although I chose to pursue a scientific career, I had the opportunity to enroll in a Computational Physics course, where I acquired a basic knowledge of C. Some years later, my curiosity led me to discover AngularJS.

By the time I finished my PhD in Chemistry, I had decided that scientific research was not for me, so I decided to become a developer.

During this last year, while studying the appropiate subjects for obtaining my qualification, I also delved deeper into the Java language, and in a few months acquired the Oracle Certified Associate, Java SE 8 Programmer Certification.

In June, I resolved to dive into the professional world and work as a developer while, at the same time, concluding my training. Emergya Digital offered me an internship which after 3 months evolved into a full-time job as Assistant Developer.

At this moment, I invest most of my efforts at my job -which I greatly enjoy- and my official developer course, but I'm also preparing myself for the Java SE 8 Programmer II exam, which would grant me the Oracle Certified Professional, Java SE 8 Programmer Certification.
