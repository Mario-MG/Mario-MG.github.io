My story

I've had an interest in programming from a very young age, and I've learnt a little bit of this and that by myself. Although I ended up choosing to pursue a scientific career, by the time I finished my PhD in Chemistry, I had come to the conclussion that scientific research was just not for me. That's when I decided to become a professional developer.

During 2020, while studying the appropiate subjects for obtaining my qualification, I also delved deeper into the Java language, and in a few months acquired the Oracle Certified Associate, Java SE 8 Programmer Certification.

Soon after, even though I hadn't finished my studies yet, I ventured to dive into the professional world and sent my résumé to Emergya. They offered me an internship, which after three months evolved into a full-time job as Assistant Developer. Four months later, I was promoted to Junior Developer.

I love my job at Emergya, where I'm part of a wonderful team (made up of wonderful people) who develop and maintain a virtual assistant for one of the top insurance companies in Spain.

If you want to learn more about me, feel free to contact me at any of the websites listed below or via email. You can also download my résumé in English and in Spanish.
